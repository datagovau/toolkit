Accreditation

Accredited Integrating Authorities

Integrating Authorities undertaking high risk data integration projects involving Commonwealth data for statistical and research purposes must be accredited.

The following organisations have been accredited by the Cross Portfolio Data Integration Oversight Board against the interim accreditation scheme.

Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS)

Date accredited: 24 April 2012
Accreditation application summary: Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS)
Accreditation application summary (pdf):ABS - application for accreditation.pdf
Contact details:

  • Visit the ABS web site at www.abs.gov.au
  • Email ABS: client.services@abs.gov.au
  • Contact your nearest ABS Office

Need more information?

The Statistical Data Integration involving Commonwealth Data website includes information on statistical data integration involving Commonwealth data including the interim accreditation scheme.

Commonwealth data integration accreditation has recently undergone a period of review. The interim Data Integration Accreditation Subcommittee Secretariat can provide information on the governance and application process. Please email dsdg-coord@pmc.gov.au
