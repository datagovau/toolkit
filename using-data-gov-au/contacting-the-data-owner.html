Contacting the Data Custodian

Contacting the Data Custodian

As the hosting platform for open data, we cannot edit or guarantee the quality and timeliness of data as it is owned by the data custodian.

Please note: The DGA support team will not respond to queries requesting further information on datasets.

If you have questions or need additional clarification on a specific dataset, please contact the organisation responsible for the data. The contact details can be located on the dataset page under Contact Point. If there is no direct contact, there will be prompts to assist you in locating the original data source contact.

You will also see on the data set page that there is an option to ‘Ask a question about this dataset.’ By clicking this link, you can complete the pre-populated prompts and send your query directly to the relevant organisation.
